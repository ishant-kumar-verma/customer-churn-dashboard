# 📊 Customer Churn Intelligence Dashboard

> **Predict • Analyze • Retain Customers**

An executive-level Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn, identify high-risk segments, estimate revenue at risk, and support data-driven customer retention strategies.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges for subscription-based businesses. This dashboard transforms customer data into meaningful business insights, enabling decision-makers to understand **who is leaving, why they are leaving, and what actions can reduce churn.**

Built with an executive-first design approach, the dashboard combines interactive visualizations, KPIs, and AI-inspired insights into a single, intuitive report.

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Highlights

- 📈 Executive KPI Cards
  - Total Customers
  - Churn Rate
  - Revenue Lost
  - High Value Customers
  - Customer Health Score

- 📑 Contract Churn Analysis

- 💳 Payment Method Churn Insights

- 🤖 Executive AI Insight Panel

- 🎛 Interactive Slicers
  - Contract
  - Internet Service
  - Partner
  - Payment Method

## 💼 Business Questions Answered

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Which contract type experiences the highest churn?
- Which internet service has the highest churn rate?
- Which payment methods contribute most to customer loss?
- How much revenue is currently at risk?
- Which customer segment should be prioritized for retention?

## 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 👥 Total Customers | Total customer base |
| 📉 Churn Rate | Percentage of customers who left |
| 💰 Revenue Lost | Estimated revenue at risk due to churn |
| ⭐ High Value Customers | Customers with high business value |
| ❤️ Customer Health Score | Overall customer retention health |
